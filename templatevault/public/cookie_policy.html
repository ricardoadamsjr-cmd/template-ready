Cookie Policy

How We Use Cookies

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website, in accordance with U.S. federal law and the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA).

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors interact with the site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to operate. They enable core features such as page navigation, security, and access to protected areas.

Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the site. We may use aggregated analytics to improve functionality, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user experience.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and choices — such as language, theme, or region — so the site feels consistent and personalized.

Advertising & Marketing Cookies

If used, these cookies help deliver relevant ads and measure campaign performance. You may opt out of targeted advertising as required under the VCDPA.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • To operate and secure the website
  • To analyze traffic and usage patterns
  • To remember user preferences
  • To improve performance and user experience
  • To support optional marketing or advertising features

4. Your Rights Under Virginia Law

If you are a resident of Virginia, the VCDPA grants you specific rights regarding your personal data collected through cookies and similar technologies.

  • Right to Access: Request confirmation of whether we process your personal data.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal data collected about you.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Opt Out: Opt out of targeted advertising, profiling, or the sale of personal data.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided in our Privacy Policy. We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third‑party cookies
  • Clear existing cookies
  • Receive alerts before cookies are stored

Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Third‑Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third‑party services integrated into our site (e.g., analytics tools, embedded media, or advertising partners). These providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our practices. The “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.
